TeamWork

Teamwork is the collaborative effort of a group to achieve a common goal or complete a task in an effective and efficient manner. Teamwork is seen within the framework of a team, which is a group of interdependent individuals who work together to achieve a common goal.

Data scientist is a job in the form of combining computer science (programming), statistics, and mathematics which aims to collect, interpret, and analyze large structured and unstructured data sets. A data scientist usually works in a team to collect various data and information.
